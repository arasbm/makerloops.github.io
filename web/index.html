Makerloops Festival

July 18th 2015, 10am to 5pm
207 Victoria Street West
Free! Everyone is welcome. Stay in the loop Sign up as a maker

What is it?

Makerloops festival is an annual celebration of maker culture in Kamloops. This year, the event is free for both makers (people presenting their creations and skills at the event) as well as attendees. This is a great opportunity to meet some of the very talented local makers. You can expect to see 3D printers, a laser cutter, CNC machines, welders, wood working, and many other old and new technologies in action. We are hoping to keep it fun and people of all age and background are welcome!

When?

Saturday July 18th 2015 from 10am to 5pm. Feel free to join us for the whole day or drop in for just a few minutes to check things out.

Where?

At the new location of Kamloops Makerspace at 207 Victoria Street West. Look for the white building with green trim right accross from Sticky's Candy. There is no parking available at the makerspace, but you may find parking at nearby businesses.

Who?

You! We will try to have activities and workshops for a diverse audience. Between 3D printers, CNC machines, old school wood working techniques, chainmaille, electronics, puzzles, games, etc. you are bound to find something amusing! Whatever your interests and skill levels are, we hope that you will enjoy this event.

Sponsors

This event, much like anything else we do at Kamloops Makerspace, is organized entirely by volunteers. We appreciate any support we can get. If you like to sponsor this event please send an email to info@kamloopsmakerspace.com or call 1-778-654-4611
